    Staking & Liquidity

    Staking and Liquidity Options¶

    Loompay's Staking & Liquidity program allows users to lock or commit USD-denominated assets or supported crypto tokens to earn rewards, unlock card and account benefits, and contribute liquidity to the Loompay ecosystem.

    The program operates on two complementary tracks:

    1. Staking Assets:

      Lock USD stablecoins or supported crypto tokens (e.g., USDC, USDT, LOOM) to earn passive rewards and unlock premium card features.

    2. Liquidity Contribution:

      Participate in liquidity pools that power Loompay‚Äôs global spend infrastructure ‚Äî bridging crypto-to-fiat payments securely and transparently.

    Liquidity Pool Option ‚Äî Crypto-backed Spending Credit¶

    1. Overview¶

    The Liquidity Pool Option lets users stake crypto as collateral and instantly access USD credit for real-world spending ‚Äî without selling their assets.

    Loompay‚Äôs liquidity engine automatically manages LTV ratios, risk thresholds, and liquidation safety via real-time on-chain monitoring.

    üí° In short: You stake crypto, Loompay gives you USD to spend ‚Äî safely, instantly, and transparently.

    2. How It Works¶

    • User selects a supported crypto (e.g., BTC, ETH, SOL).
    • The system calculates a Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratio and displays the available USD credit.
    • USD credit is instantly deposited to the Loompay Wallet/Card.
    • Continuous monitoring ensures collateral safety ‚Äî if asset value drops below threshold, partial or full liquidation is triggered.
    • User can repay anytime; collateral unlocks automatically upon full repayment.

    • Stake 1 BTC ($110,000) ‚Üí Receive $66,000 credit (LTV 60%)
    • If BTC drops below $93,500 (‚àí15%), auto-liquidation secures your position.

    Funding Source & Capital Safety¶

    Loompay‚Äôs credit liquidity comes from two primary sources:

    1. Liquidity Providers (LPs):

      Institutional and retail partners who stake USD stablecoins (USDC/USDT) into the Credit Liquidity Pool (CLP).

    2. Protocol Reserve:

      A dynamic on-chain treasury that absorbs temporary volatility and ensures real-time settlement for user withdrawals and card spend.

    üíº All credit advances are backed 1:1 by collateralized assets or liquidity reserves, ensuring system solvency and transparency.
